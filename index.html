Suresh Raina

About:

Suresh Raina (born 27 November 1986) is an Indian former international cricketer. He occasionally served as stand-in captain for Indian men's national cricket team as well as in IPL for Chennai Super Kings during the absence of the main captain, MS Dhoni

Achievements:

First ever cricketer to reach 5,000 runs in IPL. Holds the record of most no. of catches (107) in the Indian Premier League. Second after Chris Gayle and the first Indian player to hit 100 sixes in the IPL series. First Indian batsman to score centuries in all three formats of the game. Top run scorer in IPL with 4540 runs.

Trivikram

About:

Trivikram Srinivas is a dialogue writer-turned-director in the Telugu film industry. He completed his primary and secondary education in Bhimavaram. Trivikram married the niece of Sirivennala Seetha Rama Sastry. Trivikram and comedian Sunil graduated from the same college (DNR College) in Bhimavaram.

Achievements:

Trivikram Srinivas is an Indian screenwriter and director famous for his works in Telugu Cinema. Hailed by his admirers as "Guruji" and "Maatala Maanthrikudu" or the "Wizard of Words", he is famous for his witty and thought provoking dialogues. Born as Akella Naga Srinivasa Sarma, he started his career as a dialogue and scriptwriter for many hit movies like Swayamvaram (1999), Nuvve Kavali (2000), Nuvvu Naaku Nachchav (2001) and Malliswari (2004) to name a few. He debuted as director with the critically acclaimed movie Nuvve.. Nuvve... (2002).

About:

Dansari Anasuya (born 9 July 1971), commonly known as Seethakka, is an Indian politician currently serving as a cabinet minister for Panchayati Raj & Rural Development, Rural Water Supply, Women and Child Welfare in Government of Telangana. She represents Mulug assembly constituency in the Telangana legislative assembly.
